Building tools for sea kayakers

3 Raccoons is a Washington State software company run by paddlers, for paddlers.

About

3 Raccoons LLC builds Current Events, a trip-planning app that brings tidal currents, tides, wind, swell, and marine weather into a single view. Search across locations, filter by conditions, and plan with confidence.

The Team

Paddlers building for paddlers.

Graton Gathright

Product Vision & Backend Engineering

Graton is an ACA Level 3 Coastal Kayaking Instructor and Body Boat Blade coach who thrives in tide races, surf zones, and rock gardens along Washington's coast and Vancouver Island. He shaped the product vision for Current Events and owns the backend engineering. He holds a PhD in Economics from UC San Diego and previously spent seven years as an applied scientist at Amazon and Haus.
