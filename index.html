About

3 Raccoons LLC is a Washington State software company. We build Current Events, a trip-planning app for sea kayakers.

Current Events

Current Events brings tidal currents, tides, wind, swell, and marine weather into a single view. Search across locations, filter by conditions, and share trip plans with your paddling group.
